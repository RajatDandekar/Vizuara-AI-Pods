# Certification Practice Exam

*A full-length 60-question practice exam mirroring the real Claude Certified Architect — Foundations test format, with detailed answer explanations and auto-scoring.*

---

## How This Exam Works

This practice exam covers all five domains of the Claude Certified Architect — Foundations certification, weighted to match the real exam:

| Domain | Weight | Questions |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Agentic Architecture & Orchestration | 27% | 16 |
| Tool Design & MCP Integration | 20% | 12 |
| Claude Code Configuration & Workflows | 18% | 11 |
| Prompt Engineering & Structured Output | 20% | 12 |
| Context Management & Reliability | 15% | 9 |

Each question is multiple choice with four options. After submitting your answers, the notebook auto-scores your results, shows which questions you got right and wrong, and provides detailed explanations for every answer — including why the correct answer is correct and why each distractor is wrong.

---

## Recommended Approach

1. **Set a timer for 90 minutes.** The real exam is timed, and practicing under time pressure builds the right instincts.

2. **Do not look up answers.** The point is to identify gaps in your knowledge, not to get a perfect score on the practice test.

3. **Review every explanation**, even for questions you got right. The explanations often contain nuances and edge cases that deepen your understanding.

4. **Track your domain scores.** If you score below 70% in a domain, go back to that pod's notebooks and article before retaking the exam.
